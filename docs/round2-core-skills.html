Why These Skills Matter

Mastering these core interface skills will make you more efficient in your daily work. You'll learn how to:

  • Navigate quickly using keyboard shortcuts and the Tell Me feature
  • Customize your workspace to match your workflow
  • Access Business Central from any device, anywhere
  • Ensure your documents look professional and meet compliance requirements
Pro Tip

Take time to personalize your role center and learn the keyboard shortcuts. The 15 minutes you invest now will save you hours over the coming months. The Tell Me feature (Alt+Q) is particularly powerful once you know what you're looking for.
