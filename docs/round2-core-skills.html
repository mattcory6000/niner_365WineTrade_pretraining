üìù 365WineTrade Notes: Document Customization Considerations

365WineTrade adds many fields to Business Central, but not all automatically print on standard reports:

Fields requiring report customization:

  • Delivery Instructions - does not print on standard BC reports by default
  • Pickup Address (on Purchase Orders) - not included on standard BC purchase order reports
  • Various 365WineTrade-specific fields may need to be added to your document layouts

Document Worksheets for batch processing:

  • 365WineTrade includes a Document Worksheet feature for batch emailing documents
  • Requires setup: Email Report Setup, Email Bodies (templates), Document Worksheet Templates
  • Note: Batch printing from Document Worksheets is not available in BC Cloud
  • Enables sending hundreds of order confirmations, invoices, or statements at once

Your implementation team will work with you to customize document layouts to include the 365WineTrade fields relevant to your business needs.

Why These Skills Matter

Mastering these core interface skills will make you more efficient in your daily work. You'll learn how to:

  • Navigate quickly using keyboard shortcuts and the Tell Me feature
  • Customize your workspace to match your workflow
  • Access Business Central from any device, anywhere
  • Ensure your documents look professional and meet compliance requirements
Pro Tip

Take time to personalize your role center and learn the keyboard shortcuts. The 15 minutes you invest now will save you hours over the coming months. The Tell Me feature (Alt+Q) is particularly powerful once you know what you're looking for.
