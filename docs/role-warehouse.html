Warehouse Role Track

Master warehouse management, bins configuration, and inventory processes. These modules are essential for warehouse managers, inventory coordinators, and operations staff handling wine storage and fulfillment.

Before You Start: Complete Rounds I-III and consider the Trade/Sales track first. Understanding how sales orders work will help you grasp the picking and shipping processes.

  • Warehouse documents and flows
  • Receiving, putting away, picking, and shipping

Warehouse Skills Checklist

After completing this track, you should be able to:

  • Understand warehouse management flows in Business Central
  • Process warehouse receipts from purchase orders
  • Put away received inventory into bins
  • Create and process warehouse picks from sales orders
  • Process warehouse shipments
  • Set up and manage bins, zones, and locations
  • Configure bin capacities and restrictions
  • Perform inventory counts and adjustments
  • Use cycle counting for inventory accuracy
  • Handle wine-specific scenarios (temperature zones, vintage tracking, FIFO)
