Key Concepts You'll Master

Role Centers

Your personalized home page designed for your specific job role. Shows KPIs, activities, and quick access to common tasks.

Navigation Menu

Access to all lists, pages, and reports organized by business area (sales, purchasing, inventory, finance).

Tell Me Search

Quick search tool (Alt+Q) to find any page, report, or action without navigating through menus.

Personalization

Customize your workspace by hiding, showing, and rearranging fields and actions to match your workflow.

What Comes Next

After completing this module, you'll understand:

  • How to log in and navigate the Business Central interface
  • What a Role Center is and how to use it effectively

  • How to find help and access the Tell Me search feature

You'll be ready to move on to Round II: Core Skills, where you'll dive deeper into the user interface and learn advanced navigation techniques.

üìù 365WineTrade Notes: What Will Be Different

When you transition to 365WineTrade, you'll notice these additions to the Business Central interface:

365WineTrade Menu:

  • A new top-level menu item will appear in your navigation with access to wine-specific features
  • Key menu sections include: Setup, Accounts Receivable, Credit, Purchasing, Inventory Allocations, Chargebacks, Document Worksheets, and Integration

Wine Setup:

  • The central configuration page for all 365WineTrade features
  • Located under 365WineTrade > Setup > Wine Setup
  • Controls settings like: units of measure (bottle/case), pricing behavior, compliance options, and more
  • Your implementation team will configure this during setup

Additional Setup Pages:

  • States and Sales Regions
  • Order Types and Sales Order Charges
  • Brands, Producers, Vintages, Varietals, Labels
  • Credit Classes and Order Review Categories
  • Allocation Codes and Chargeback Settings

The core Business Central navigation you're learning now stays the same - 365WineTrade adds to it rather than replacing it.
