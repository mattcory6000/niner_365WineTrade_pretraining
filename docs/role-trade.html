Trade/Sales Skills Checklist

After completing this track, you should be able to:

  • Navigate and understand the item master data structure
  • Create new items using templates
  • Create purchase orders for wine shipments
  • Process receipts and match invoices
  • Create sales quotes and convert them to orders
  • Process sales orders and create shipments
  • Apply customer-specific pricing and discounts
  • Understand allocation concepts (365WineTrade-specific)
  • Use vintage tracking effectively (365WineTrade-specific)
