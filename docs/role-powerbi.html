Wine Industry Power BI Reports

Common Power BI dashboards and reports for wine businesses:

  • Sales Performance Dashboard: Track sales by region, varietal, price point, and sales rep
  • Inventory Aging Report: Monitor inventory age by vintage, identify slow-moving SKUs
  • Customer Analysis: Segment customers by purchasing patterns, identify wine club trends
  • Allocation Tracking: Monitor allocated wine distribution and availability
  • Profitability Analysis: Gross margin by product category, supplier, and customer
  • Producer Performance: Compare sales and profitability across wineries and suppliers
  • Warehouse Metrics: Bin utilization, picking efficiency, and fulfillment KPIs
  • Vintage Performance: Compare sales and margins across different vintage years

Tip: 365WineTrade can provide starter Power BI templates designed specifically for wine distributors and importers.

Power BI Skills Checklist

After completing this track, you should be able to:

  • Understand Power BI components and their roles
  • Connect Power BI Desktop to Business Central data
  • Import data from multiple sources (BC, Excel, databases)
  • Create data models with relationships between tables
  • Write basic DAX formulas for calculated measures
  • Create effective visualizations (charts, tables, cards)
  • Design professional dashboards with filters and slicers
  • Publish reports to Power BI Service
  • Share dashboards with appropriate permissions
  • Schedule automatic data refresh
  • Build wine-industry-specific reports (sales, inventory, allocations)
