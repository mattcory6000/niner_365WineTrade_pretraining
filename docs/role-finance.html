üìù 365WineTrade Notes: Wine Industry Dimensions

365WineTrade offers unique dimension management for wine businesses:

  • Dimension Synchronize - automatically sync dimension values with wine entities (Brands, Vintages, Labels, Varietals, Producers)
  • Brand Dimensions - set default dimensions on Brands that flow to all items
  • Producer Dimensions - set dimensions on Producers that flow to Brands and Items
  • Dimension Group Codes - consolidate large chargeback credit memos by grouping transactions by dimensions

When a new Vintage or Brand is created, the corresponding dimension value is automatically created - eliminating double-entry and ensuring consistency across financial reporting.

üìù 365WineTrade Notes: Wine Industry Posting

365WineTrade adds specialized posting configurations for wine operations:

  • Chargeback Posting Setup - defines GL accounts for Depletion Allowances, Bill Backs, and Bad Bottles by Gen. Bus./Gen. Prod. Posting Group combinations
  • Vendor Posting Group Overrides - chargebacks can post to different GL accounts than regular purchases
  • Payment Terms Overrides - chargebacks can use different payment terms than standard vendor transactions
  • Case-Amount Rounding Precision - prevents rounding inconsistencies in case/bottle calculations

These ensure wine-specific transactions (chargebacks, samples, allocations) post correctly to your GL.

üìù 365WineTrade Notes: Collections & Credit Management

365WineTrade enhances credit and collections with wine industry tools:

  • Credit Classes - categorize customers by credit status with "On Hold" flags
  • Credit Review Dashboard - customer-centric view showing all orders, balances, and credit status
  • Historical Credit Class tracking - audit trail when customers go on/off hold
  • State Liquor Authority integration - automatically flag delinquent licenses or inactive accounts
  • Credit Hold triggers Sales Order Reviews - orders automatically stop for approval when credit issues exist

When a customer's Credit Class changes, ALL their open orders automatically update - ensuring credit holds are enforced system-wide.

üìù 365WineTrade Notes: Important Distinction

‚ö†Ô∏è Note: Business Central's "General Ledger Allocations" (Module 7 above) is about distributing costs across GL accounts and dimensions.

365WineTrade's "Inventory Allocations" is a completely different feature - it manages allocating physical inventory to customers, states, salespeople, or brands. Inventory Allocations creates commitments for who can purchase specific quantities of allocated items.

Both features exist independently:

  • GL Allocations = financial cost distribution (BC standard)
  • Inventory Allocations = physical inventory reservation system (365WineTrade add-on)

Your implementation team will cover Inventory Allocations separately during 365WineTrade training.

After completing this track, you should be able to:

  • Navigate and maintain the chart of accounts
  • Create and configure dimensions for reporting

  • Configure posting groups to automate GL entries

üìù 365WineTrade Notes: What Finance Needs to Know

When transitioning to 365WineTrade, Finance will encounter these key processes:

  • Chargeback Processing - generating vendor credit memos for Depletion Allowances, Bill Backs, and Bad Bottles
  • Depletion Allowance Ledger Entries - tracking amounts owed by vendors for volume-based rebates
  • Beverage Tax calculations - automated federal and state excise tax on alcoholic beverage sales
  • Sample Billback processing - recovering costs from vendors for samples given to customers
  • Allocation Journal posting - when inventory allocations create/consume reserved quantities

These specialized transactions will have dedicated GL accounts in your chart of accounts. Your implementation team will configure the Chargeback Posting Setup to ensure proper GL flow.
