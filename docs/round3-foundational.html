üìù 365WineTrade Notes: Sales and Purchasing Configuration

365WineTrade adds extensive configuration requirements beyond standard Business Central:

Sales Configuration (Wine Setup and related pages):

  • Order Types - templates that preset location, charges, and approval requirements
  • Sales Order Charges - automatic minimum order fees and split-case charges
  • Order Review Categories - define approval workflows for credit, samples, price changes, etc.
  • Sales Regions - determine which items can be sold to which areas (usually by state)
  • States - control co-op pricing, allocation, and compliance requirements
  • Credit Classes - categorize customers by credit status with hold flags

Wine Industry Master Data:

  • Brands and Producers (with optional dimension synchronization)
  • Vintages, Varietals, Labels, Appellations
  • Unit Sizes (750ml, 1.5L, etc.)
  • Alcohol Types, Wine Colors, Origin Regions

Purchasing Configuration:

  • Blanket Purchase Order Synchronization settings
  • Purchase Consolidation number series
  • Chargeback Setup - for Depletion Allowances, Bill Backs, and Bad Bottles
  • Vendor posting group and payment terms overrides for chargebacks

Your implementation team will help you configure these during your 365WineTrade setup. Understanding the standard BC sales/purchasing concepts first makes these wine-specific enhancements easier to grasp.
