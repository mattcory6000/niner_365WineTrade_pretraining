BC Navigation

For detailed information about Business Central navigation including login procedures, role centers, company switching, and help features, please refer to the complete Markdown guide or the original documentation from Wendy.

Key Navigation Concepts
  • Role Center: Your personalized home page designed for your job role
  • Tell Me (Alt+Q): Universal search for finding pages, actions, and reports
  • Navigation Menu: Organized by business area (Sales, Purchasing, Inventory, Finance)
  • Personalization: Customize your workspace to match your workflow

Filter Criteria Quick Reference

Symbol Example Finds
.. (Interval) 1000..2000 Numbers 1000 through 2000
| (Or) 10|25 Numbers 10 and 25
<> (Not equal) <>0 All numbers except 0
> (Greater than) >100 Numbers greater than 100
< (Less than) <2500 Numbers less than 2500
>= (Greater or equal) >=15 Numbers 15 or higher
<= (Less or equal) <=2000 Numbers 2000 or lower
& (And) >55&<100 Numbers between 55 and 100
'' (Exact match) 'booth' Exactly "booth" (case-sensitive)
@ (Case insensitive) @booth* Text starting with "booth" (any case)
* (Wildcard) *wine* Text containing "wine" anywhere
? (One character) Park?r Parker, Parkur, etc.
