365WineTrade Note: Powerful Filtering for Wine Data

Filtering is especially powerful in 365WineTrade when working with:

  • Wine inventory - filter by vintage, varietal, region, appellation
  • Allocated products - filter by customer allocation status
  • Compliance data - filter by TTB requirements, state regulations

Pro tip: Save your common filters as Views so you can quickly switch between them without recreating filters each time.

Deep Dive Available

For comprehensive filtering techniques, symbols, and examples, see the Sorting, Searching, and Filtering Lists section in the Appendix. It includes a complete reference table of filter symbols and criteria.

365WineTrade Extensions for Sales & Purchasing

Sales and purchasing in 365WineTrade include wine-specific features:

  • Allocation management: Track and sell allocated wines
  • Vintage tracking: Maintain history and availability by vintage
  • Case configurations: Handle mixed cases, bottle splits, and case breaks
  • Pricing by vintage: Different pricing for different vintages of the same wine
  • Compliance checks: Automatic validation of licensing and shipping restrictions

When you complete this module, you'll understand the foundation—then we'll show you how 365WineTrade extends these capabilities for the wine industry.
